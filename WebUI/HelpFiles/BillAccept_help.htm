Counter Level Operation

At counter level, the clerk receives either Payment bills or Receipt bills from DDO or any authorized messenger. A payment bill is received in form of Voucher and a receipt bill is received in form of a Challan. A payment bill can be a salaried bill or a non-salaried bill. Every DDO, submitting the bill for getting payment. All non-salaried bill has a defined budget. In case of non-salaried bills, budget verification is done.
