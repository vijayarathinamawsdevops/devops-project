Auditor Level Operation

Once the bill is thorough with Counter Level processing and budgeting, the bill is received for auditing. Auditing is done at three levels. The bills Passing & Accepted for payment at the auditor level are called Vouchers which are used for Accounting purpose.

If the bill is up to the “Query” criteria, Auditing clerk makes some more entries for the bill in the system and prepares the Pay Order. Pay Order along with the bill is passed to the Superintendent for second level of auditing.

If the bill is not up to the “Query” criteria, Auditing clerk makes and objection and sends the bill to the auditor level-2.

Superintendent does the second level of auditing. He verifies the auditing done at first level and doesn’t do any significant data entry. A superintendent can also reject a check if he/she observes a mistake. The bill is sent to the auditor -3 levelr with an objection mark.

Third level of auditing is done by PAO or ASTO. PAO or ASTO verifies the signatures of the DDO and passes the bill. If any objection found in the bill, the bill will be return to the counter. The counter level clerk makes an entry in Return Bill Register and handovers the bill with objection note to the DDO or messenger.

If auditors have approved the bill, bill is sent to cheque printing. After the cheque is printed, cheque is sent to counter. The counter level clerk makes and entry in the Scroll and handovers the cheque to the DDO/Messenger.
