Cheque Operation

If the bill is approved in the auditing phase, cash is handed over to the DDO/Authorized messenger if the treasury office is of type banking, otherwise a cheque is released by receiving the token. Every payment is recorded by making an entry in the scroll, which is passed on to the Input section for detail data capture. If there is any objection that is raised against the bill, it is recorded in the Return Bill Register. Cheque or cash is paid from STO or from DTO.
